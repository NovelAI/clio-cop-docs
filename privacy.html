Privacy Policy

Effective 2026-06-11. clio-cop ("the bot", "we") is an automated moderation bot operated by Anlatan within our Discord server. This policy explains, to you as a member of that server, what data the bot accesses and how it is handled.

1. Scope

This policy covers the clio-cop bot as it operates in the Anlatan Discord server. It applies to members of that server whose posted images the bot scans. The bot is not made available for use in other servers.

2. What we access

The bot operates on messages posted in channels it can see. Specifically:

  • Image attachments. The bot reads the image files attached to messages in order to score them for scam content. Accessing attachments on ordinary messages requires Discord's Message Content intent.
  • Message and author identifiers. The message ID, channel ID, and — only when a message is actioned — the author's user ID, so moderators can review the decision.

The bot does not read, parse, log, or store the text of messages, nor does it use the Presence or Server Members intents.

4. Storage and security

  • The bot maintains no database and keeps no long-term store of member content. Image processing is in-memory and transient.
  • The only retained record of member content is moderation evidence, which lives inside the server's own private moderator-only channel within Discord.
  • Credentials (the bot token) are stored as host environment configuration, never in source code, and are not shared with third parties.
  • The bot does not sell, rent, or share data with any third party. Processing is local to the bot host; no analytics or advertising services are used.

5. Calibration data

To tune detection accuracy and minimize false positives, we may periodically sample recent images from designated channels. These samples are processed in memory, reduced to non-reversible numerical embeddings, and the underlying images are discarded — they are never written to disk or retained.

6. Children's privacy

The bot is intended for use in communities that comply with Discord's minimum age requirements and is not directed at children under those requirements.

7. Your choices

The bot only acts on images you post in the server, so it processes no data about you unless you post image attachments. You may leave the server at any time. If you were actioned and believe it was in error, or you have questions or requests regarding data the bot has handled relating to you (including deletion of moderation evidence), contact the server's moderators or us at juan@anlatan.ai.

8. Changes

We may update this policy as the bot evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with a new effective date.
